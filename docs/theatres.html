THEATRES

Alex the Jester, with his immense warmth, charm and zany shenanigans, will blow the roof off your next theatrical production.


Your audience will be completely enthralled with his cornucopia of delightful surprises.

Alex the Jester packs many facets of “audience appeal” into one show:

  • Daring Stunts
  • Mind-Bending Sight Gags
  • Dazzling Visual Spectacle
  • Musical Capers
  • Unbelievable Physical Feats

Added to this are vibrant colors, audience interaction, and lavish costumes. This Massachusetts-based entertainer has enchanted audiences around the world.

“What a show!  You’re clever, funny, amazing and totally captivating. It was terrific having you as part of the 23rd annual Philadelphia International Children’s Festival.  Audiences, both young and old, from all walks of life, filled each performance to capacity.  In fact, we had to turn some people away!” –Roy A. Wilbur, Director of Philadelphia International Children‘s Festival


“What awesome creativity” –Jon Yanofsky, Executive Director, Paramount Center for the Arts, NY


“Alexander sold out 10 out of 11 shows.  Our audiences couldn’t get enough of him” –Beth Rose, Alaska Center for the Performing Arts

Audiences often remark how they could not take their eyes off him, not even for a moment.  Unlike a speaking performer, all the humor and emotions are conveyed with his tone of voice, expressive face and flexible limbs. And once audiences start chuckling, they don’t want to look away and risk interrupting that delicious flow of endorphins and pleasurable hormones that the body releases during a good laugh! Scientific studies have shown that laughter really is the best medicine to reduce the pains and stresses of daily life. Besides that, Alex the Jester is just plain fun!

Alex the Jester will not only get you and your audience laughing, but his mischievous character connects in a deeper way.    

Alex the Jester’s humor is multi-layered.  While parents are enjoying watching the fool pine for love, their kids are cracking up at the sight gags. It’s a magicalexperience when three generations are laughing at once. Alex the Jester involves the audience, not only in egging him on and cheering, but in assisting him with his pranks (when the spirit moves them).

As part of his performance, Alex the Jester tastefully recognizes the presenter and any sponsoring agencies for the important behind-the-scenes work that they do.
